About These Metrics

MAE
Mean Absolute Error — weighted-average absolute deviation between forecast and actual price (EUR/MWh). Lower is better.
RMSE
Root Mean Squared Error — penalises large errors more than MAE. Lower is better.
Dir. Acc.
Directional Accuracy — fraction of hours where the forecast correctly predicted whether the price moved up or down relative to the previous hour. Higher is better; 50% is random.
Coverage
Percentage of actual prices that fell within the ensemble P25–P75 band. Ideally ~50%.
R²
Coefficient of determination from the most recent model training run (higher is better; 1.0 = perfect fit on training data). This reflects model fit quality, not live forecast accuracy.
Archived
Number of daily forecasts stored in the last 30 days with comparable actuals.
Hours
Total hourly slots where both a forecast and an actual price are available.
Currency
All prices are in EUR/MWh as published on the ENTSO-E Transparency Platform. Zones outside the eurozone (Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Poland, etc.) are included — ENTSO-E and Nordpool both report cross-border prices in EUR/MWh.
